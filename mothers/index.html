Community Temple Church Mother's

Honoring Our Church Mothers

Mother Joe

Alpha: June 4, 1932 - Omega: October 17, 2023

Until We became much older, Mom, There was just no way We could know, The sacrifices you must have made, As you watched your children grow, We didn't consider how tired you were, At the end of every long day, Or appreciate the meals you cooked... We were clueless, We guess you could say. We never thought of the time you spent, Tending to all of our needs, Putting us first each step of the way.. And that's how your life story reads. Now, without question, We understand, A phrase that still rings in my ear, "There's a special place in heaven for Moms?" Looking back... it makes it all the more clear!

Mary Ann Brand

July 9, 1954 - Sunset: April 3, 2017

These are words that you will never hear. These are words whisper low and clear. You told us you were tired, But yet still strong. You told us don't worry, For worrying is wrong. Coming home was one thing you mentioned, And we waited for that day. But the coming home we looked for, We didn't know it was this way. We wish you were here and going to miss you so dear, But God loves you better, And with Him We know you have no more fear.
~ Your Loving Children

Mother Ruth

Earthly Arrival: September 18, 1953

Heavenly Departure: October 19, 2013

You can only have one mother, patient kind and true; No other friend in the world, will be the same to you. When other friends forsake you, to mother you will return, For all her loving kindness, she asks nothing in return. As we look upon her picture, sweet memories we recall Of a face so full of sunshine, and a smile for one and all. Sweet Jesus take this message, to our dear mother up above; Tell her how we miss her, and give her all our love.
~ Love Your Children
