2025 Events ▼

Men’s Program

August 17th, 2025

Our Men’s Program was held at 3:30 PM with men in black suits, white shirts, and red ties. Minister Jacob Wilson from Bethlehem Missionary Baptist Church delivered a powerful message, and the day was filled with worship, fellowship, and brotherhood.

Back To School Program

July 26th, 2025

On July 26 we had our Back to School program titled "A Time Like Us". Refreshments were served, and the kids were given free school supplies. The event was filled with joy, music, and a strong sense of community spirit. Parents and children alike enjoyed the atmosphere, and every child left feeling prepared and encouraged for the new school year. We thank everyone who helped make this day special.

Pentecost Celebration 2025

June 6th – June 8th

Our 2025 Pentecost Celebration was a powerful and joy-filled three days of worship, fellowship, and community.

On June 6th, we began with a powerful evening of worship, the Word, and uplifting songs that set the tone for the weekend.

On June 7th, we celebrated water baptism, rejoicing as 8 souls publicly declared their faith: ✨ Tony Fulton Sr. ✨ Tony Fulton Jr. ✨ Antonio Mose ✨ Riliegh Mitchum ✨ Jaylen Battle ✨ Naloni Elmore ✨ Maisha Murphy ✨ Shannon Singletary. It was a day filled with gospel music, delicious free food, and plenty of laughter. The children especially enjoyed the water slide and bounce house.

On June 8th, we closed our celebration with a Spirit-filled Sunday service, giving thanks for all God has done.

Family & Friends Day

May 4th, 2025 | 3:00 PM

We gathered for an unforgettable Family & Friends Day, celebrating the gift of loved ones and the bonds we share in Christ. The afternoon was filled with joy, laughter, and heartfelt moments.

Special thanks to our guest speaker, Pastor Lee Roy Epps of Wilson Chapel Church, for delivering an inspiring word that touched every heart. It was a day of unity, love, and spiritual encouragement — a true blessing to all who attended.

Palm Sunday & Resurrection Sunday

April 13th, 2025 & April 20th, 2025

Palm Sunday was a time of reflection as we gathered for a meaningful service featuring scripture readings and a powerful message honoring Jesus' journey.

The following week, we celebrated the Resurrection of Jesus — a joyous occasion marking His victory over death.

Men's Meeting & Teri Musician Program

March 29-30th, 2025

Saturday, March 29 — The weekend began with the Community Temple Men's Meeting and Breakfast. A time of fellowship, encouragement, and spiritual growth brought the men together, while a complimentary breakfast provided a warm start to the day.

Sunday, March 30 — The celebration continued with a Musical Tribute to Anthony A. Gillard (Teri). Spirit-filled performances by choirs and groups created an uplifting afternoon of worship, gratitude, and joyful celebration.

Black History Program

January 16, 2025

We celebrated Black history with songs of praise, and with teaching and preaching, honoring the legacy, resilience, and contributions of African Americans throughout history. The event also featured inspiring stories, cultural performances, and a call to continue the fight for equality and justice.

Hall of Faith Service

October 25-27, 2024

The Hall of Faith service is a special ceremony honoring the legacy of church members who have passed, particularly the beloved church mothers who played vital roles in the ministry. This service allows the congregation to come together in gratitude, sharing memories and celebrating the enduring impact of these women. Through stories, prayers, and song, we remember their faith and dedication, knowing their legacy continues to inspire and strengthen our church community.
