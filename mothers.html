Community Temple Church Mother's

"These are the fallen mothers of Community Temple Church who held a very important position in the ministry. Words can't express the gratitude of Community Temple Church for our fallen mothers, who will always be remembered and never be forgotten." Hall of faith Service (Oct. 27th, 2024)

Mother Pearlie Mae McCormick

Sunrise: October 13th, 1940 - Sunset: July 22, 2005

We thought that you would like to know that all our thoughts go where you go because of you being the ~Mom~ we wanted you to be; may God forever be close to thee. ~ Your Loving Children

Mother Lottie Dukes

Sunrise: July 3, 1927 - Sunset: September 13, 2007

To Our Mother Our lives seem so strange without you here, There was never a time when you weren't near. But as we look around us, you'll never be gone, For you left us with memories that will always live on. To some you may be forgotten, to others a part of the past, But to those who loved and lost you, your memory will last. There is a chair at the table that can never be filled, Since God took you home and your voice has been stilled We see you in many places along life's way, Sometimes we can hear you in something we say, We know in our hearts that God knew what was best, When He silenced your pain and took you home to rest. ~ Love, Your Children

Mother Jacqueline Gillard

Sunrise: June 4, 1932 - Sunset: May 17, 2009

Little Did We Know Mom, little did we know that morning that God would call your name. In life we loved you dearly, in death we do the same. It broke our hearts to lose you, but you did not go alone. For part of us went with you the day God called you home. You left us peaceful memories, your love is still our guide. And though we cannot see you, you are always on our side. Our family chain is broken and nothing seems the same. But as God calls us one by one, the chain will link again.

Mother Ruth Brockington

Earthly Arrival: September 18, 1953 - Heavenly Departure: October 19, 2013

You can only have one mother, patient, kind, and true; No other friend in the world will be the same to you. When other friends forsake you, to mother you will return, For all her loving kindness, she asks nothing in return. As we look upon her picture, sweet memories we recall Of a face so full of sunshine, and a smile for one and all. Sweet Jesus take this message to our dear mother up above; Tell her how we miss her, and give her all our love. ~ Love Your Children ~

Mother Mary Ann Brand

Sunrise: July 9, 1954 - Sunset: April 3, 2017

To Our Mother These are words that you will never hear. These are words whispered low and clear. You told us you were tired, but yet still strong. You told us don't worry, for worrying is wrong. Coming home was one thing you mentioned, And we waited for that day. But the coming home we looked for, We didn't know it was this way. We wish you were here and going to miss you so dear, But God loves you better, And with Him, we know you have no more fear. ~ Your Loving Children ~

Mother Josephine Fulton Brand

Alpha: June 4, 1932 - Omega: October 17, 2023

Until we became much older, Mom, There was just no way we could know, The sacrifices you must have made, As you watched your children grow. We didn't consider how tired you were, At the end of every long day, Or appreciate the meals you cooked... We were clueless, we guess you could say. We never thought of the time you spent, Tending to all of our needs, Putting us first each step of the way... And that's how your life story reads. Now, without question, we understand, A phase that still rings in my ear, "There's a special place in heaven for Moms?" Looking back makes it all the more clear!
